Operation Modes

Flexible Notification Types

Choose the optimal approach for your email notifications with our dual-mode architecture designed for different business scenarios.

📢
Single Mode
Broadcast identical content to multiple recipients efficiently. Perfect for company announcements, newsletters, and mass communications.
  • Unified message delivery to all recipients
  • Support for TO, CC, and BCC distribution
  • Advanced filtering from inventory database
  • Automatic recipient deduplication
  • Configurable batch processing with delays
  • Comprehensive delivery tracking
🎯
Multi Mode
Generate personalized emails based on dynamic filter conditions. Ideal for targeted notifications, departmental updates, and segmented messaging.
  • Individual emails per filter condition
  • Dynamic template personalization
  • Field-based content substitution
  • Advanced segmentation capabilities
  • Scalable multi-notification processing
  • Granular recipient targeting
